about the yoga shala

The Yoga Shala was founded in 2023 out of a need for a well-rounded yoga space. This studio is more than just a place to exercise. It is a space to hold you when you want to learn, grow, cry, breathe, find challenges, or find peace. We offer a variety of classes every week so you can truly experience all aspects of a yoga practice. The aim of our studio is for you to go beyond the physical and really experience the full mind-body-breath connection.

frequently asked questions

I've never done yoga before. Do I need experience to come to this studio?

No. We offer a variety of classes to meet the needs of all students, and within classes instructors will offer various options to advance or modify parts of the practice.


What should I wear?

Wear comfortable clothing that allows you to move and stretch easily. You don't need to bother with any 'trendy' yoga clothes - just wear what feels good for you! Shoes and socks will be removed prior to class.


Do I need to bring anything with me to take class?

Personal preference. If you have a yoga mat and/or props you love, feel free to bring those as well as a water bottle. If not, we offer mats and props for free and have a water station in the studio.
